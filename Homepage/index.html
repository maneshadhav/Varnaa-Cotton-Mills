Our Legacy

Varnaa Cotton Mills is created in 1971 a beacon of excellence and innovation in the textile industry. For decades, Varnaa Cotton Mills has been at the forefront of transforming fabric into not just garments, but stories woven with tradition, creativity, and sustainability and is currently upheld by KRISHNAKUMAR G.

History

Initially started by Natesa Mudhaliyar in the year 1971,and later ventured into textiles in 1974. Natesa Mudhaliyar a remarkable individual whose contributions to the textile industry have left an indelible mark for 4 generations and would continue
